GAME MECHANICS

Surviving the overrun hospital requires mastering Zoi's physical limits and combat skills. Escape the Zongus outbreak through these core systems.

STAMINA SYSTEM

Attacking, performing combos, and sprinting will drain Zoi's stamina. Restore it by killing and picking up enemies to stay in the fight.

HOSPITAL STAGES

Escape through four challenging stages. Battle Zongus (zombies) and mad scientists while solving puzzles and collecting keys.

RECOVERY & HEAL

After every wave of enemies, a gate will open to provide a health box or med kit. Use these to restore Zoi's health.

CHECKPOINTS

Progress is saved at checkpoints. If Zoi dies, she will respawn at the last activated location to continue the mission.

OBJECTIVES & BOSS FIGHTS

Face powerful bosses at the end of each stage to earn great rewards like med kits and new weapons.

  • Level Up: Fight enemies to increase Zoi's strength as you progress.
  • Resource Gathering: Collect useful resources hidden throughout the hospital.
  • Variety of Enemies: Encounter different enemy behaviors that make each battle a unique challenge.
