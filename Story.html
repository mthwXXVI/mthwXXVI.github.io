Story

Every month, groups of scientists and heavily armed military personnel arrive in Zoi’s hometown. Their presence brings fear to the entire community because of a cruel rule they enforce: every family must give at least one child to them. No one knows the true reason behind this demand, but the consequences of refusing are severe. Because of this, the people of the town have no choice but to obey, sacrificing their children to protect the rest of their families.

Zoi, a teenage boy living with his parents in this quiet town, eventually becomes one of those chosen to be taken. Seeing the fear and helplessness in his family’s eyes, Zoi decides to surrender himself willingly so that his family will not suffer the consequences. With a heavy heart, he leaves his home, not knowing where he will be taken or what will happen to him.

Along with other children, Zoi is transported to a hidden and isolated location far from civilization. Beneath the surface lies a massive underground laboratory where the scientists conduct dangerous and mysterious experiments. The children are locked inside cold and dimly lit cells, treated as test subjects rather than human beings. Day after day, the scientists perform various experiments on them, hoping to create something beyond human capability.

Many of these experiments fail, leaving behind victims that are no longer fully human. The laboratory slowly becomes a place filled with fear, suffering, and strange creatures born from these failed experiments.

One day, everything suddenly changes. A failed experiment manages to break free from containment. The creature begins attacking anything in its path, causing chaos throughout the underground facility. Alarms echo through the halls, emergency lights flash, and panic spreads among the scientists and guards.

In the middle of the chaos, Zoi finds himself among the few survivors left alive. Realizing this may be his only chance, he decides to escape the laboratory before it’s too late. The underground facility is filled with locked doors, dangerous creatures, and hidden paths that lead deeper into the unknown.

With nothing but his determination to survive and the hope of seeing his family again, Zoi begins his desperate journey to escape the laboratory. But escaping will not be easy.

Lore (World Background)

Long before the events of the game, the world was already facing a silent crisis. A mysterious phenomenon began affecting human biology, causing unusual mutations and unexplained illnesses in different parts of the world. Governments and secret research organizations began searching for a solution, believing that human evolution could be artificially accelerated to adapt to the changing environment.

This led to the creation of several hidden research facilities across the country. These laboratories were built in secret locations to conduct experiments that were too dangerous and unethical to be revealed to the public.

The underground laboratory where Zoi is taken is one of these facilities. Its purpose is to develop a new form of human evolution—one that can survive the harsh conditions of the future. However, the experiments are unstable and unpredictable. Many test subjects transform into hostile creatures after failed procedures.

To continue their research, the scientists secretly collect children from nearby towns, believing that younger bodies are more adaptable to experimental treatments. Over time, countless children have disappeared into these facilities, never returning home.

Unknown to the outside world, the laboratory has become a prison filled with failed experiments and dangerous mutations. Now, after a catastrophic containment breach, the entire facility has fallen into chaos. And somewhere inside this underground nightmare, Zoi is trying to escape.
