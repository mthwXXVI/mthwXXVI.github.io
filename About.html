ABOUT THE GAME

ZYMERA STUDIO

Overview

Zoi: Escape is an adrenaline-pumping 2D Platformer that blends Zombie Survival and Sci-Fi Horror. As a player, you must escape a dark and dangerous underground laboratory teeming with failed experiments and biohazard threats. Using quick reflexes and tactical thinking, you must navigate traps and fight off zombies to make it out alive.
